About PRAVAHAI

PRAVAHAI – meaning "the flow" – is our college's flagship annual techno-cultural festival that brings together the brightest minds and most talented artists from across the country. With over 20+ events spanning technology, science, arts, music, dance, and more, PRAVAHAI is the ultimate platform for students to showcase their skills, discover new passions, and make lifelong connections.

Featuring industry-leading speakers, mind-bending competitions, dazzling performances, and electrifying workshops, PRAVAHAI 2026 promises to be the most spectacular edition yet!
