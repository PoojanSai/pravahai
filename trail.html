About PRAVAHAI

PRAVAHAI – meaning "the flow" – is our college's flagship annual techno-cultural festival that brings together the brightest minds and most talented artists from across the campus. With over 10+ events spanning technology, science, arts, music, dance, and more, PRAVAHAI is the ultimate platform for students to showcase their skills, discover new passions, and make lifelong connections.

Featuring industry-leading speakers, mind-bending competitions, dazzling performances, and electrifying workshops, PRAVAHAI 2026 promises to be the most spectacular edition yet!

Event Schedule

Three days packed with unforgettable moments

10:00 AM

Opening Ceremony

Grand inauguration with cultural performances, lamp lighting, and welcome addresses from the Chief Guest and college dignitaries.

📍 Main Auditorium
11:00 AM

ChatBot Challenge: Hackathon Kickoff

Problem statement revealed on the spot; teams must use their own laptops to build a prototype in VS Code within 1–2 hours, with AI tools allowed for reference

📍 Tech Block A
12:00 PM

AI Trivia Quiz : Future of AI

Three rounds—Basic (general computer MCQs), Intermediate (moderately difficult AI-oriented questions), and Advanced (deep AI/tech questions with visuals); teams of 2–5 members.

📍 Seminar Hall
2:00 PM

AI Escape Room

Individual participation; a 4-level challenge where each level is unlocked by solving a set of questions.

📍 Open-Air Stage
3:00 PM

Art Battle

Three rounds—Real vs Reel (identify real or AI-generated images), Face Fusion (guess the revealed image using hints), and Guess the Song (complete missing lyrics with hints like hero/heroine)..

📍 Main Stage
10:00 AM

Opening Ceremony

Grand inauguration with cultural performances, lamp lighting, and welcome addresses from the Chief Guest and college dignitaries.

📍 Main Auditorium
11:00 PM

AIML Model Design Contest

Teams of 2–4 members must build their models in VS Code based on the provided problem statements, with AI tools allowed only for reference.

📍 Design Studio
12:00 PM

Reverse Engineering

Individual or teams of 1–2 members compete across 3 rounds, with hints provided in each round.

📍 Main Stage
2:00 PM

Food Without Fire

Participants bring their own ingredients and prepare a delicious dish without using fire, with the tastiest creation winning.

📍 Conference Hall
3:00 PM

Fun- In

Enjoy exciting activities including online games like Free Fire, BGMI, Ludo, network crash challenges, stalls, and other fun games.
